Members




We are a group of enthusiastic students that help support the mathematical community on the campus. Throughout the year, we organize and host academic events, programs and social activities to help encourage collaboration, active participation and teamwork amongst the students. We are dedicated to making mathematics accessible and fun to all BITSian students, and to enhance the experience of undergraduate mathematics learning.
